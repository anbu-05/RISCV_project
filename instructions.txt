jump and branch instructions are encoded as x2. i.e., if you wanna jump to location 40, type 20. if you wanna offset by 32 locations, type 16

1: addi x1, x0, 12
4: addi x2, x0, 16
8: add x6, x1, x2
12: sub x7, x2, x1
16: addi x5, x0, 2
20: sll x8, x7, x5
24: beq x8, x2, 8

40: addi x20, x8, 13
44: lui x28, 128
48: addi x29, x0, 2047
52: bne x20, x8, 16


80: addi x21, x8, 16
84: jal x31, 20
